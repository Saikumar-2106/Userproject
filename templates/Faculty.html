About IT Faculty

Dr. Ramesh Naidu Balaka, Professor - Head of the Department
       Dr. Ramesh Naidu Balaka has been working as Professor in the Department of Information Technology at Aditya Institute of Technology and Management (A), Tekkali, permanently affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University - Gurajada, Vizianagaram, Andhra Pradesh, India. He completed M.Tech in Computer Science and Technology from Andhra University, Vishakhapatnam, India. He awarded a Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in Computer Science and System Engineering from Andhra University, Vishakhapatnam, India. He served almost 20 years of experience in teaching and research. He guided 30 Under Graduate and 5 Post Graduate academic projects. Presently, he is a life member of ISTE, CSI, and ACM. He published 4 Indian Patents in Indian Patent Journal, Govt. of India and granted 1 patent in the year 2024 by Intellectual Property (IP) India, Government of India. He is attended more than forty workshops in various places and also he attended various IEEE conferences with in INDIA and abroad. He also attended one international IEEE conference, Bejing, CHINA. He published around 25 Research papers in various reputed National and International Journals like Springer, Elsevier and other. He also wrote one textbook titled a “Multimodal Biometric Authentication System”. He was sanctioned by a Two-Week FDP on “Machine Learning using Python Programming” in 2021. He Recognized as Research Guide ship in Andhra University, Visakhapatnam, India in the year 2023.
